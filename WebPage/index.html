About Cupboardly

Overview

Cupboardly is an Android application developed as a senior project at Pennsylvania Western University. It addresses a common problem in both home and business kitchens: keeping track of ingredients and recipes effectively.

Whether you're managing ingredients for personal cooking or running a food business, Cupboardly simplifies the process by providing an organized, user-friendly interface for ingredient and recipe management.

Key Features

Ingredient Tracking

Track ingredients with quantities, expiration dates, prices, and dates added. Organize by individual batches and easily swap between units.

Recipe Management

Manage recipes with ingredient lists and instructions. Calculate total recipe cost based on ingredient prices in your inventory.

Recipe Cost Analysis

Automatically calculate how much each recipe costs based on the purchase prices of ingredients used.

Unit Conversion

Easily swap between different units including gallons, grams, cups, and pounds for flexible ingredient management.

AI Recipe Scanning

Add recipes by taking pictures of physical recipes. The app parses the information for quick recipe entry.

Inventory Deduction

When starting a recipe, the app automatically deducts the required ingredients from your inventory.

Data Management

Export and import your ingredients and recipes as database files for backup and sharing purposes.

Availability Alerts

Get notified when you don't have enough ingredients to make a specific recipe.

How It Works

Ingredient Screen

  • View a complete list of all ingredients with total quantities
  • Click on an ingredient to expand and see individual batches
  • Each batch displays expiration date, quantity, price, and date added
  • Convert between different measurement units
  • Edit or delete ingredients and batches

Recipe Screen

  • View all recipes in an organized list
  • Click to expand and see ingredients, instructions, and total recipe cost
  • Add recipes manually or by scanning a photo
  • Edit recipes as needed
  • Start a recipe to automatically deduct ingredients

Installation Instructions

Step 1: Transfer the APK (if on a computer)

If you're downloading on a computer, connect your Android device to the computer via USB cable. Transfer the downloaded APK file to your device's storage. If you're already on your Android device, skip to Step 2.

Step 2: Install the APK on Your Device

  1. Open the file manager on your Android device and locate the cupboardly.apk file
  2. Tap the APK file to begin installation
  3. You may see a security warning about installing from an unknown source. Tap Install anyway or Allow to proceed
  4. Wait for the installation to complete
  5. Once installed, you're ready to launch Cupboardly
